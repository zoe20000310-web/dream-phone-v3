Principle This web prototype uses your phone’s motion and orientation sensors to estimate body rhythm, breath-like movement, touch, and motion. These signals are sent to the main dream page in real time, where they affect drifting, breathing, splitting, and entering the tunnel.

Apple Watch is not required for this version. A future enhanced version could connect real heart-rate data through Apple Watch / HealthKit, but this page currently does not record audio, video, or real medical heart data.
